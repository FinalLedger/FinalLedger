What is Final Ledger?

Who is Final Ledger for?

Life is full of uncertainty & challenges. But with Final Ledger, EVERYONE can take control and play a part in keeping on top
of these vital circumstances. Creating a Final Ledger Account allows you to store all of your vital information that your loved
ones will need access to in times of uncertainty and poor health or worse.

How does it work?

Final Ledger stores provided data for users in a manner that can be utilized at the user owner's discretion on an
individual basis. After account creation, you have access to all areas of your personal profile. Here you can customize
your profile and send emails to specific people who you want to have the ability to read your information. No one can see
your personal information unless you want them to!

Why should I use it?

With Ease of Use and Safety being at the heart of our values, you can feel confident that at the time
when you no longer can help yourself, your family can step in and help you or themselves with little to no added stress
in a very stressful time.

Data Security

Final Ledger uses top of the line security implementations. Keeping your data where it belongs.
